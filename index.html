How to Play Pong

  • Move Paddle: Move your mouse up/down on the left to control your paddle.
  • Pause/Unpause: Press the Spacebar or click Pause button.
  • Restart: Click Restart to reset scores and the game.
  • Goal: Try to bounce the ball past the AI paddle on the right!
  • High Scores: Top 3 Player-AI scores are tracked.
